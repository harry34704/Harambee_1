Our Story

Established in 2010, Harambee Accommodation has been at the forefront of providing premium student accommodation solutions. Our name "Harambee" reflects our commitment to bringing students together in a supportive community environment.

We understand the importance of a conducive living environment for academic success. That's why we've created spaces that combine comfort, security, and modern amenities to support your educational journey.

Our Mission

To provide safe, comfortable, and affordable accommodation that enhances the student experience and supports academic excellence.

  • Foster a diverse and inclusive community
  • Provide modern amenities and facilities
